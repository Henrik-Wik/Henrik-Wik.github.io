About Me




Hello, my name is Henrik Wik.

I am a third-year student in the Analytical Finance program at Mälardalen University. It is a mathematics program with a focus on financial engineering. Some of my favorite courses I have taken are Portfolio Theory, Analytical Finance with MATLAB, and my degree project in mathematics, therefore, I am very interested in working in these areas. I will graduate in June 2023, so I am now looking for my first full-time job. I currently live in Bålsta but would be happy to move if I find a good job.

My program is also international, so all my courses are in English, and my fellow students often speak in English. I also have some knowledge in bookkeeping, invoicing, and administration, both from my education and as I have worked a bit at the alarm company Tele W with administration and documentation. I also spent 4-5 years on the MScEng. program in industrial economics before switching to Analytical Finance as I wanted to work more with financial economics. Therefore, I have a lot of experience in group work and presentations with many different people, and I also have a lot of management courses that others in my program do not have.

In addition to my skills, I am very thorough, analytical, structured, and constantly try to develop myself. For example, I do a lot of strength training in my spare time and write my own exercise programs in Excel to keep track of my progress. I spend some time organizing my schedule and am always on time when it comes to schoolwork. As a mathematician, I also like problem solving and learning new things. I enjoy group work at school, and often also try to take responsibility and make sure everything goes as it should. In my free time, I sit a lot at the computer, I have a good grasp of the stock market and like to invest myself when I am not playing games with friends.
